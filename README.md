# 🚀 LifeOS

### Transform Your Daily Routine Into A Digital Experience

![LifeOS Banner](assets/images/banner.png)

> **LifeOS** is not just a productivity dashboard.
>
> It is a modern digital command center designed to help users organize their life, track goals, maintain journals, manage tasks, analyze productivity, and stay motivated—all inside a visually immersive and futuristic interface.

---

## ✨ Vision!

Most productivity applications focus on only one aspect of life.

LifeOS brings everything together.

Whether you're planning your day, recording memories, tracking habits, monitoring goals, or simply checking the weather before starting work, LifeOS becomes your personal digital companion.
